Humidity


Humidity Description

There does not seem to be a clear correlation between city latitude and humidity. There does seem to be some clustering of values at the highest percentages of humidity and the greatest latitudes. It could be said that as latitude increase, it is somewhat likely that that the percentage of humidity also increases. The correlation between these two variables, however, is not strong and it would unlikely that there would be and predictive validity between these variables.
