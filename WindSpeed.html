Windspeed Description

Much like the scatterplot for cloudiness, there does not seem to be any correlation or relationship between windspeed and latitude. The majority of the windspeed values are between 0 and 10 miles per hour, which tells us that windspeed is generally quite low in most cities. There is no pattern or association with measurements of latitude for each of the cities given.
