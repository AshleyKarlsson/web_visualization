MaxTemp Description

The correlation between maximum temperature and latitude is the strongest among all of the visualizations generated. There is a clear parabolic trend in which as latitude increases from -60 to about 20, the maximum temperature also increases and as latitude continues to increase from about 20 to 80, the maximum temperature decreases. This gives the plot a curved trend line that peaks around the latitude of 20 with the loawest points at about -60 and 80.
